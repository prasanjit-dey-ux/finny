# Finny Design System

## Brand
- **App Name:** Finny
- **Tagline:** Your smarter way to save and grow
- **Persona:** Smart, friendly, trustworthy personal finance advisor

## Colors
- **Primary Dark:** #1E3A5F (deep navy)
- **Primary Mid:** #4A90D2 (medium blue)
- **Primary Light:** #87CEEB (sky blue)
- **Accent Gold:** #F5A623 (golden - coins, highlights)
- **Accent Green:** #2ECC71 (positive, income)
- **Accent Red:** #E74C3C (expense, alert)
- **Background:** #F0F4F8 (light gray-blue)
- **Card BG:** #FFFFFF
- **Text Primary:** #1E3A5F
- **Text Secondary:** #64748B
- **Text Light:** #94A3B8

## Gradients
- **Header Gradient:** #1E3A5F → #4A90D2
- **Card Gradient:** #4A90D2 → #87CEEB

## Typography (Expo uses system fonts)
- **Display:** Bold, 28-32px
- **Heading:** SemiBold, 20-24px
- **Body:** Regular, 14-16px
- **Caption:** Regular, 12px

## Spacing
- Base unit: 4px
- Screen padding: 20px
- Card padding: 16px
- Gap between cards: 12px

## Border Radius
- Cards: 16px
- Buttons: 12px
- Chips: 20px

## Shadows
- Cards: elevation 2-4, subtle

## Style
- Clean, modern fintech
- Dark navy headers with white text
- White cards on light background
- Gold accents for financial elements
- Green/red for positive/negative values
